get to know me

I am a software engineer located in Atlanta, GA. I played football at both Alabama and Auburn and had aspirations of playing in the NFL but after 2 shoulder surgeries, an mcl tear, popped tendons and appendix surgery I finally decided to hang up the cleats and begin my life (yes I really had no life). After discovering there is life after football, I moved out to California to begin my career as a management consultant helping companies with their business processes in order to fit into the software systems they had. After just over a year in California I decided to take a break from the corporate life and pursue my interests in real estate but over that time I realized how much I enjoyed software and wanted to get back into it, except this time I wanted to develop it from the ground up. After that, I made the decision to fully devote my time to creating awesome software and learning everything I possibly can in the process.
